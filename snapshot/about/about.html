material-design-lite-gwt (MDL-GWT) aims at bringing the Material Design experience to your GWT projects. At the heart of MDL-GWT is the lightweigh css and javascript library material-design-lite (MDL) which is an accurate transcription of the material design experience for the web. MDL provides a set of UI components that are best suited for static web pages.

MDL-GWT builds upon the infrastructure provided by MDL to bring the material design lite look and feel to the GWT community. MDL-GWT provides a GWT wrapping for the original components and extends their capabilities making them more suitable for dynamic environments like single page applications.
